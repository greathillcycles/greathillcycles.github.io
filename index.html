About Us

Hi! I’m Alex and I’m the face behind Great Hill Cycle Mechanics. I love bikes — like really love bikes — so if I can give your bike a bit of TLC to make you love bikes even half as much as I do, we’re onto a winner!

I’ve worked in the cycling industry for almost ten years, working on a huge variety of bikes including mountain, road, gravel & hybrids. I’m a big believer in good, proper bike maintenance making cycling more enjoyable & accessible to all — so here at GHCM, we’re more than happy to take on any maintenance or repair job, no matter how big or small!
